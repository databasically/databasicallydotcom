PROBLEM

Children's educational programming has hit an all-time low because it is not engaging or interesting. CDN came to us with a concept to allow viewers to explore documentary content in the order that most interested them so they would stick with content through multiple segments.

Required services

SOLUTION

We built a full-featured support platform that has everything small businesses need to offer awesome customer support, and nothing that they don’t. Using Ruby on Rails to build the server-side ticketing functionality and Node.js for real-time chat, we crafted a beautiful interface that users rave about.
