Announcing: Lowdown, a Cucumber feature editing webapp

Aug 25 2009

An original application completed for Rails Rumble 2009. Lowdown helps keep your development project on track by helping developers, designers, project managers and owners all focus on satisfying stakeholders. Built along with Sean Cribbs + Scotty Moon + Paul du Coudray While leading the competition after judges' voting ended, we slipped to 4th place after public voting and ended up taking the "Best Appearance" category, which is fantastic.
