Google Redirect Virus

Aug 6 2010

Otherwise known as the search engine redirect virus. The symptoms you'll experience are:
  1. Go to your search engine.
  2. Search for something.
  3. Click on link to website.
  4. Get taken away to something completely different!
I've experienced this on Google, Yahoo, MSN, Altavista, and many others. It's important to note that most antivirus programs CAN NOT fix this.
