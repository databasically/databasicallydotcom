Railsconf 2016 - Kansas City - May 4, 5, 6

Events Surrounding Railsconf 2016

Hi! If you're coming to Railsconf 2016 in Kansas City and you're arriving a day early or late, this is the place to find something to do.

Who are you?

My name is Wes Garrison. I grew up in Kansas City and I want you to see something other than the inside of a conference center. So, I'll be coordinating a lot of different events so you can see the sights. Plus, new Ruby Friends are always great!

How does this work?

  • Follow @wesgarrison for more timely updates as we get closer.
  • Look at the events below.
  • Send suggestions to @wesgarrison
  • For popular events, I'll send out specific arrangements, ticket details, transportation.
  • For general attractions, I'll help coordinate and connect interested parties.
  • Get on the mailing list for each event. Check your email for a confirmation!
